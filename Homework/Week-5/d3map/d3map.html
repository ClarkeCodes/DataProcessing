Design choices
For this map I chose to display two types of data for each country on mouseover. To be able to visually compare the amount of refugees in each country, I chose to color the countries by the amount of refugees per 1.000 people in each country. Additionally on mouseover, you can see the total amount of refugees in a country. For countries where there is no data, I show only the country name and the fact that there's not data available.

On mouseover, the border of the country changes color to hightlight your selection. I chose to not use a changing fill color on mousover, so the original values I gave to each country by their color are still visible at all times.

Data from Wikipedia.
